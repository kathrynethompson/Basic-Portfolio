About Me

"Hi there! My name is Kathryne Thompson but I go by KT. I am currently a student at the University of Texas at Austin coding bootcamp. I am currently studying development but my primary professional goal is to become a software or electromechanical engineer. I've always been passionate about computers and technology since a very early age. My primary hobby is building computers. Although I love building, taking apart, and rebuilding computers...it tends to become an extremely expensive hobby after a while. So why not make a career out of it? After years of learning the ins and outs of hardware I wanted to dive into the intermediate behind the scenes aspect of how the computer itself runs...cue development."

"I don't look like your typical computer geek...its the only thing I've ever truly excelled at. I hope to surpass boundaries and change the game. Not only for myself, female engineers, but everyone in the IT field."
